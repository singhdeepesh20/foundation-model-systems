## Overview

This repository is a structured, research-driven exploration of **modern Artificial Intelligence systems**, with a primary focus on **foundation models, transformer architectures, and intelligent systems engineering**.

The goal is to move beyond surface-level usage of libraries and instead develop a **deep, first-principles understanding** of how AI systems are designed, trained, optimized, and deployed.

This repository reflects a mindset of:

* Building from scratch
* Understanding system-level design
* Experimenting rigorously
* Thinking in terms of scalability and real-world impact
